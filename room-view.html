The Provenance

Completed over 14 months in Elena Voce's Milan studio. This piece was the centerpiece of the 'Silent Tide' exhibition at the 2023 Venice Biennale Fringe. It has never been held by a private collector, moving directly from the artist's personal archive to Artisane.
