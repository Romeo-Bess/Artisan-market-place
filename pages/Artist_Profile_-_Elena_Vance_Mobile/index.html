Elena Vance

Oil on Canvas Abstract Expressionism

"Art is the silence between the noise of modern life."

Based in London, Elena Vance explores the intersection of memory and landscape through luminous, layered oil paintings. Her work resides in the 'White Cube' space of emotion, using minimal palettes to evoke maximal resonance. A recipient of the 2023 Curator’s Choice Award, her pieces are collected globally.
