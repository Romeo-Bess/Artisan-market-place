Terms & Conditions - Auction Protocol

1. Auction Terms of Service

By participating in an Artisane Gallery auction, you acknowledge that you are entering into a legally binding contract. All bids are final and cannot be retracted. The Artisane platform serves as a curated facilitator for fine art transactions between international collectors and artists.

Bidding Increments

Bidding follows a standardized progression. Increments are determined by the current high bid: $0 - $1,000 ($50), $1,000 - $5,000 ($250), $5,000+ ($500). Artisane reserves the right to adjust increments during live sessions to maintain momentum.

Buyer's Premium

A buyer's premium of 15% will be added to the hammer price of each lot and is payable by the purchaser as part of the total purchase price. This premium supports our curation, authenticity verification, and global white-glove logistics.

Payment & Automated Settlements

Winning bidders are required to complete payment within 48 hours of the auction close. By registering, you authorize Artisane to automatically charge your pre-authorized payment method for the total amount (Hammer Price + Premium + Shipping) immediately upon the conclusion of the auction if other payment is not received.

Authenticity Guarantees

Artisane guarantees the authenticity of all works for a period of 5 years from the date of sale. Every piece is accompanied by a blockchain-backed Certificate of Authenticity (CoA) registered to the new owner's profile.
